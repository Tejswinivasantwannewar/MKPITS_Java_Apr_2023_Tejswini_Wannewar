Department Of Neurology

Nephrology is a medical subspecialty that deals with the function of the kidneys and disorders that affect them. Kidneys are the body’s complex filtration units. Any change or failure in the structure or physiology of the kidney can lead to acute or chronic renal (kidney) illnesses, which can have a significant impact on the overall body’s function. The Department of Nephrology at Adhikari Lifeline Hospital is equipped with cutting-edge technology, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a team of devoted and professional nephrologists and paramedical staff. Our nephrologists in Boisar work hard to provide the finest care possible to patients with acute or chronic kidney disease. Our centre offers a 24/7 dialysis service under the supervision of Nephrologists and the most advanced Haemodialysis machines. Adhikari Lifeline’s Dialysis Unit is fully equipped with efficient cardiac monitoring, central oxygen supply and other emergency treatment facilities for critically ill patients.n days a week, owing to well-trained staff and best cardiologists in Boisar.
